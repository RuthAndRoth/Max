Second Life/OpenSim LSL Script for avatar relaxed hands at idle with bentohandrelaxedP1 animation
Sourced from https://github.com/RuthAndRoth/Extras/tree/master/Animations
CC-BY-4.0

Place the bentohandrelaxedP1.bvh animation and bentohandrelaxer.lsl into the avatar body part which contains the hands.

Note that some avatar HUDs can automatiocally apply a hand idle animation so a specific hand relaxer may not always be needed
